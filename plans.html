FAQ
Can I test the signals before upgrading to VIP?
Yes. Join the free channel and follow the signals on a demo account first. Take your time — we'd rather you join with confidence than rush in.
How do I receive signals?
Signals are posted directly in the Telegram channel in real time. You'll get a notification the moment one is live.
What broker do I need?
We recommend Exness or JustMarkets — both support M-Pesa, low spreads and MT5. Open through our partner links and unlock free VIP access.
What markets do you cover?
Primarily XAUUSD (Gold), major Forex pairs, and indices like NASDAQ and US30. Crypto setups occasionally when conditions are right.
How many signals per day on VIP?
4–7 premium signals daily, spread across Asian, London and New York sessions. Quality over volume — every call is one we've committed to.
Is mentorship suitable for beginners?
Absolutely. Sessions are tailored to your current level. Whether you're brand new or have been trading for a while, we meet you where you are.
