// How to Post Signals
Post signals directly to your Telegram channel @leetraderarc using this format and they'll appear on the website automatically:
XAUUSD BUY Entry: 2341.50 SL: 2328.00 TP: 2365.00 Session: London
✦ Pair: XAUUSD, EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY, BTCUSD, NASDAQ, US30, etc.
✦ Direction: BUY or SELL (first line after the pair)
✦ Session: Asian, London, or New York
✦ Messages that don't follow this format are shown as general updates, not signal cards.
✦ To mark a signal as TP Hit, reply to it in Telegram with: TP HIT ✓
